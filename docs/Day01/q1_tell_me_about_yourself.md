# Day 01 - Behavioral Interview

## Question 1 - Tell Me About Yourself

### Importance

★★★★★

### Frequency

★★★★★

---

## Interviewer's Intent

The interviewer wants to:

- Understand your background
- Evaluate communication skills
- Assess relevance to the role
- Identify your strongest technical areas
- Determine whether your experience aligns with the DCS Cloud team

---

## Recommended Answer (English)

My name is Yeong-Gyu Choi, and I currently work as a Lead Platform Engineer at NSoft America.

I have over 10 years of experience across software engineering, cloud infrastructure, DevOps, and platform engineering.

Throughout my career, I have worked in a variety of environments ranging from enterprise cloud platforms to AI infrastructure and GPU-based data centers. My experience includes Kubernetes platform operations, infrastructure automation, cloud architecture, CI/CD systems, and large-scale operational environments.

Prior to joining NSoft America, I worked on AI data center and GPU infrastructure projects supporting organizations such as SK Telecom, Samsung, and SK Hynix. Those experiences gave me strong exposure to reliability engineering, large-scale operations, troubleshooting, and cross-functional collaboration.

In my current role, I lead platform engineering initiatives and help customers design and improve infrastructure platforms, automation systems, and operational processes.

What attracted me to ByteDance is the opportunity to work on infrastructure at a truly global scale. I enjoy solving reliability and operational challenges, and I believe my background in cloud platforms, Kubernetes, automation, and AI infrastructure aligns well with this role.

---

그동안 엔터프라이즈 클라우드 플랫폼부터 AI 인프라, GPU 데이터센터까지 다양한 환경에서 근무했으며 Kubernetes 운영, 인프라 자동화, 클라우드 아키텍처, CI/CD 구축 및 운영 경험을 보유하고 있습니다.

## Key Expressions

### I currently work as...

현재 ~로 근무하고 있습니다.

Example:
I currently work as a Lead Platform Engineer at NSoft America.

---

### I have over 10 years of experience in...

~ 분야에서 10년 이상의 경험이 있습니다.

Example:
I have over 10 years of experience in cloud infrastructure and platform engineering.

---

### Throughout my career...

제 경력 전반에 걸쳐

Example:
Throughout my career, I have worked extensively with Kubernetes and cloud platforms.

---

### My experience includes...

제 경험에는 다음이 포함됩니다.

Example:
My experience includes Kubernetes operations, CI/CD, and infrastructure automation.

---

### Prior to joining...

~에 합류하기 전

Example:
Prior to joining NSoft America, I worked on AI data center projects.

---

### Strong exposure to...

깊이 있는 경험을 쌓았습니다.

Example:
I gained strong exposure to reliability engineering and troubleshooting.

---

### Cross-functional collaboration

다양한 조직과 협업

Example:
The project required extensive cross-functional collaboration.

---

### What attracted me to...

제가 ~에 관심을 갖게 된 이유는

Example:
What attracted me to ByteDance is the opportunity to work at a global scale.

---

### Aligns well with this role

이 역할과 잘 맞습니다.

Example:
My background aligns well with this role.
