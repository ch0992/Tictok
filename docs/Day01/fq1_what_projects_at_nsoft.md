## Follow-up Question 1 - What kind of projects are you currently working on at NSoft?

### Importance

★★★★★

### Frequency

★★★★★

---

## Interviewer's Intent

The interviewer wants to:

- Understand your current responsibilities
- Verify the claims made in your self-introduction
- Assess technical depth
- Evaluate leadership and ownership
- Understand how relevant your current work is to the ByteDance role

---

## Recommended Answer (English)

At NSoft America, I primarily focus on platform engineering, infrastructure architecture, automation, and AI-related initiatives.

A significant part of my role involves working with customers to design and improve infrastructure platforms, operational processes, and engineering workflows. Depending on the project, this may involve cloud platforms, Kubernetes environments, CI/CD systems, infrastructure automation, and on-premises infrastructure.

More recently, I have also been involved in AI-focused initiatives, including establishing engineering standards, improving development workflows, and helping teams adopt AI-assisted engineering practices.

One aspect I particularly enjoy is identifying operational inefficiencies and designing solutions that improve reliability, automation, and engineering productivity.

Overall, my role is a combination of technical leadership, platform engineering, architecture design, and operational improvement.

## Key Expressions

### I primarily focus on...

주로 ~에 집중하고 있습니다.

Example:
I primarily focus on platform engineering and infrastructure architecture.

---

### A significant part of my role involves...

제 역할의 중요한 부분은 ~입니다.

Example:
A significant part of my role involves working with customers to improve infrastructure platforms.

---

### Depending on the project...

프로젝트에 따라

Example:
Depending on the project, I may work with Kubernetes, CI/CD, or cloud platforms.

---

### I have been involved in...

~에 참여해 왔습니다.

Example:
I have been involved in AI-focused engineering initiatives.

---

### Engineering workflows

엔지니어링 워크플로우

Example:
We improved engineering workflows through automation.

---

### Operational inefficiencies

운영 비효율

Example:
I enjoy identifying operational inefficiencies and improving processes.

---

### Engineering productivity

엔지니어링 생산성

Example:
The project significantly improved engineering productivity.
