## Recommended Answer (English)

The biggest difference is that traditional development processes were designed around humans writing code, while NStack is designed around humans collaborating with AI.

In a traditional workflow, engineers typically create requirements, implement code, open pull requests, and document only a small portion of the decision-making process.

As a result, a significant amount of engineering knowledge remains undocumented. Teams often lose visibility into why a decision was made, what alternatives were considered, or how a particular implementation evolved.

With AI-assisted development, this challenge becomes even more significant because much of the design and implementation process happens through conversations with AI.

NStack treats those interactions as first-class engineering artifacts.

Instead of focusing only on source code, NStack captures the entire engineering process, including requirements, prompts, implementation plans, architectural decisions, issues, outcomes, and lessons learned.

The framework is built around three concepts: Rules, Skills, and Workflows.

Rules define engineering standards and constraints.

Skills provide reusable engineering capabilities and patterns.

Workflows define how engineers and AI collaborate to complete tasks consistently.

The goal is not simply to generate more documentation. The goal is to preserve engineering knowledge, improve collaboration, reduce duplicate work, and make AI-assisted development sustainable at team scale.

In many ways, NStack treats engineering knowledge as a product rather than a byproduct of software development.

## Korean Summary

가장 큰 차이는 전통적인 개발 프로세스는 사람이 직접 코드를 작성하는 것을 중심으로 설계되었고, NStack은 사람과 AI가 함께 개발하는 환경을 중심으로 설계되었다는 점입니다.

기존 개발 방식에서는 요구사항, 코드, Pull Request 정도만 남고 실제 의사결정 과정은 사라지는 경우가 많습니다.

특히 AI 개발에서는 중요한 설계 과정이 대부분 AI와의 대화 속에서 발생하기 때문에 더욱 심각한 문제가 됩니다.

NStack은 이러한 AI와의 상호작용 자체를 엔지니어링 산출물로 취급합니다.

단순히 코드만 관리하는 것이 아니라 다음 정보를 모두 관리합니다.

- 요구사항
- 프롬프트
- 구현 계획
- 설계 의사결정
- 이슈
- 결과
- 회고

NStack은 Rules, Skills, Workflows 세 가지 개념으로 구성됩니다.

목표는 문서를 많이 만드는 것이 아니라 엔지니어링 지식을 보존하고, 협업을 개선하며, AI 기반 개발을 조직 단위로 확장 가능하게 만드는 것입니다.
