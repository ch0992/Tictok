# Day 01 - Behavioral Interview

## Question 3 - Why are you considering leaving your current company?

### Importance

★★★★★

### Frequency

★★★★★

### Probability

Very High

This question is commonly asked by recruiters, hiring managers, and technical interviewers.

---

## Interviewer's Intent

The interviewer wants to:

- Understand your career motivation
- Assess professionalism and maturity
- Determine whether you are leaving for positive reasons
- Evaluate long-term commitment
- Understand whether this role is a logical career move

---

## Recommended Answer (English)

I would not say that I am actively looking to leave my current company.

I enjoy my current role and have had the opportunity to work on a variety of interesting platform engineering, infrastructure, and AI-related initiatives.

At the same time, I am always open to opportunities that offer new technical challenges, larger-scale environments, and opportunities for continued growth.

What attracted me to this opportunity is the chance to work on infrastructure that operates at a truly global scale.

Throughout my career, I have enjoyed building platforms, improving reliability, automating operational processes, and solving complex infrastructure problems.

I believe the DCS Cloud role would allow me to continue developing those skills while working on systems that support products used by hundreds of millions of users worldwide.

So rather than moving away from something, I would say I am moving toward an opportunity that aligns very well with my long-term interests in reliability engineering, large-scale infrastructure, and platform operations.

## Korean Summary

현재 회사를 떠나기 위해 적극적으로 이직을 준비하고 있는 것은 아닙니다.

현재 역할도 만족하고 있으며 플랫폼 엔지니어링, 인프라, AI 관련 프로젝트를 수행하며 많은 경험을 쌓고 있습니다.

다만 저는 항상 새로운 기술적 도전과 더 큰 규모의 환경, 그리고 성장 기회에는 열려 있습니다.

ByteDance에 관심을 갖게 된 이유도 글로벌 규모의 인프라를 경험할 수 있기 때문입니다.

저는 플랫폼 구축, 신뢰성 향상, 운영 자동화, 복잡한 인프라 문제 해결을 좋아해 왔으며, DCS Cloud 역할은 이러한 관심사와 매우 잘 맞는다고 생각합니다.

따라서 현재 회사를 떠나기 위해 움직이는 것이 아니라, 장기적인 커리어 목표에 더 잘 맞는 기회를 향해 나아가는 것에 가깝습니다.

## Key Expressions

### I am not actively looking to leave

적극적으로 이직을 추진하고 있지는 않습니다.

Example:
I am not actively looking to leave my current company.

---

### Open to opportunities

기회에는 열려 있다

Example:
I am always open to opportunities that align with my interests.

---

### Larger-scale environments

더 큰 규모의 환경

Example:
I am interested in working in larger-scale environments.

---

### Technical challenges

기술적 도전

Example:
I enjoy solving technical challenges.

---

### Long-term interests

장기적인 관심사

Example:
The role aligns with my long-term interests.

---

### Reliability Engineering

신뢰성 엔지니어링

Example:
I would like to continue growing in reliability engineering.

---

### Platform Operations

플랫폼 운영

Example:
The position provides exposure to large-scale platform operations.
